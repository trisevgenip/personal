Based in London, UK

Researcher,
technologist,
and builder.

I work at the intersection of technology and human cognition. Currently at UCL & Digital Trust Council, thinking about how we build tools that are good for people. My research focuses on investigating belief representations in human and artificial minds. At the DTC I am leading research that builds incentives for trustworthy AI. My background is in Cognitive Science (PhD, MSc) and Science and Technology Studies (MSc). I previously spent time at Google Deepmind, the Alan Turing Institute & the UK Department of Health and Social Care.
